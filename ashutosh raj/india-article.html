India

Commonly known as the 'Republic Of India', India is World's Largest Democracy. India is located in the south east region of asia continent. India is the seventh largest country in the world. In terms of population, It is the second largest populous nation in the world. It is sorrounded with Indian Ocean in the south, Pakistan in the west and China, Nepal and Bhutan in the north, Bangladesh and Myanmar in the east. Islands of India like Andaman and Nicobar Islands share a maritime border with Sri Lanka, Thailand, Myanmar and Indonesia
